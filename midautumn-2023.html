2023 MID-AUTUMN FESTIVAL Highland Park

2023年中秋游园

Thanks Borough of Highland Park for supporting the event.

Thanks Mayor Elsie Foster for suggesting this event.

Time: 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM 09/30/2023, Saturday
Location: Farmers Market Lot
212 Raritan Ave, Highland Park, NJ 08904 (Between S 2nd Ave and S 3rd Ave)

Celebrate a traditional Asian holiday, showcase Asian culture, and promote community engagement and unity.

庆祝传统节日，展示亚裔文化，促进社区参与和团结。

Performances

Enjoy traditional and contemporary dancing, Chinese opera musical instruments, martial arts, and Tai Chi.

Shows & Schedule

Business Activities

Explore our shop area, offering a variety of traditional food and snacks. Discover calligraphy, Chinese painting, and crafts, and learn about the techniques and history behind the culture and art. Try on traditional clothing and accessories.
